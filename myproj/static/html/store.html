Stores, also known as shops or retail establishments, are physical or virtual locations where goods and services are offered for sale to consumers. These establishments play a crucial role in the distribution and exchange of products, allowing businesses to connect with customers and fulfill their needs and desires. Stores can vary widely in size, format, and offerings, catering to different customer preferences and market niches.These traditional brick-and-mortar establishments have a physical presence in a specific location, such as shopping malls, high streets, or standalone buildings
