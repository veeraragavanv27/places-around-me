Cafes strive to create a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. They often have comfortable seating arrangements, such as sofas, armchairs, and small tables, encouraging customers to sit and enjoy their time.Coffee and tea are the most popular beverages served in cafes. A wide variety of coffee options, such as espresso, cappuccino, latte, and more, are usually available. Teas, including herbal, black, green, and flavored teas, are also commonly offered.
