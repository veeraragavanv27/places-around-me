Jewelry, also spelled as "jewellery" in British English, refers to decorative items that are worn for personal adornment. Throughout history, jewelry has been an essential part of human culture and has served various purposes, including as a symbol of social status, a form of artistic expression, a means of religious or spiritual significance, and as personal adornments.Jewelry can be made from a wide range of materials, including precious metals like gold, silver, and platinum, as well as various gemstones and minerals such as diamonds, rubies, sapphires, emeralds, pearls, and more. Additionally, there are jewelry pieces made from non-precious metals, beads, glass, wood, and other materials.
